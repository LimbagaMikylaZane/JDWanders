Siquijor Island Tour

Tour Details

Experience the mystical charm of Siquijor – an island known for its pristine beaches, waterfalls, and folklore. This 3-day tour will immerse you in nature and local culture.

Inclusions:

  • Round-trip transportation via ferry
  • 3 nights accommodation
  • Daily meals (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)
  • Guided island tour (Cambugahay Falls, Salagdoong Beach, Century Old Balete Tree, etc.)
  • Motorbike rental for a day

Duration: 3 Days

Price: ₱13,500

Details:

This tour is perfect for adventurers who want to enjoy white sand beaches, historical sites, and local delicacies in one of the Philippines’ most enchanting islands.
