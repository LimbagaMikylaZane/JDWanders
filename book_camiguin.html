Camiguin Island Tour

Tour Details

Visit the island born of fire — Camiguin. Known for its natural hot springs, waterfalls, and white sandbars, this tour offers a relaxing and scenic 3-day escape.

Inclusions:

  • Round-trip ferry and land transportation
  • Accommodation for 2 nights
  • Meals (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)
  • Tour guide and entrance fees
  • Island hopping and nature trekking

Duration: 3 Days

Price: ₱7,800

Details:

Enjoy the beauty of White Island, Ardent Hot Springs, Sunken Cemetery, and the majestic Katibawasan Falls. Perfect for those who want to relax while still experiencing adventure.
